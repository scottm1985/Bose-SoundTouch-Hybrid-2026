Manual Setup Guide

📡 MANUAL WI-FI SETUP
1. Power on the speaker.
2. Press and hold 2 and - (Volume Down) for 5 seconds.
3. The Wi-Fi indicator will blink amber (Setup Mode).
4. Connect your device to Wi-Fi: "Bose SoundTouch X".
5. Visit: http://192.168.1.1:8090
🔵 MANUAL BLUETOOTH PAIRING
1. Select Bluetooth source.
2. Hold Bluetooth/AUX button until light blinks Blue.
3. Scan and pair on your phone.

Clear List: Hold Bluetooth/AUX for 10s (Double Tone).
🔄 MANUAL FACTORY RESET
SoundTouch 10, 20, 30:
Turn the speaker on. Press and hold 1 and - (Volume Down) for 10 seconds until the indicators blink.

SoundTouch Wireless Link:
Press and hold the Control button on the back for 10 seconds until the Wi-Fi indicator blinks white.
🔌 MANUAL FIRMWARE UPDATE (USB)
1. Connect the speaker to your computer via via USB/Micro-USB setup cable.
2. Open a web browser.
3. Visit the hidden update page: http://203.0.113.1/update.html
4. Select and upload your Update.stu firmware file.
